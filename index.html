🐟 Fishery

Practical fish farming support for small and medium farmers.

🐠 Fish Species

Popular options: Rohu, Catla, Mrigal, Tilapia and Pangasius based on local market demand.

🏞️ Pond Preparation

Dry pond, remove weeds, apply lime, and fill clean water before stocking seed.

💧 Water Quality

Keep pH 7-8.5, monitor dissolved oxygen, avoid overfeeding and dirty inflow water.

🌾 Feed

Use balanced feed as per fish size and growth stage. Feed in fixed points daily.

🩺 Fish Diseases

Watch for ulcers, gasping, white patches and sudden mortality. Isolate and consult fisheries officer.

🏛️ Schemes & FAQ

Check PMMSY and state fisheries department support for pond, seed and cold-chain subsidies.

🐄 Poultry & Livestock

Simple guidance for poultry, dairy and goat-based income.

🐔 Poultry Farming

Maintain dry litter, proper ventilation and clean water for healthy growth.

🥛 Dairy Farming

Balanced ration, regular deworming and clean shed improve milk yield and animal health.

🐐 Goat Farming

Choose hardy breeds, provide shade and mineral mixture, and prevent overgrazing.

🌿 Feed Management

Mix green fodder, dry fodder and concentrate feed according to age and production stage.

💉 Vaccination

Follow local vaccination calendar for FMD, HS, BQ, PPR and poultry diseases.

🩺 Common Diseases & Schemes

Early diagnosis saves loss. Contact veterinary doctor and explore animal husbandry schemes.
